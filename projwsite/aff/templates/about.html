Martin DCX1AE

A Sitka spruce top is paired with mahogany patterned high-pressure laminate (HPL) back and sides for a very classic looking Martin Dreadnought. This 14-fret guitar cutaway features a hand-rubbed top and neck finish with a Richlite fingerboard and bridge and Fishman Sonitone electronics.

Yamaha Dreadnought

The perfect entry level guitar. The comfortable dreadnought body, professional-grade tonewoods, and fast-playing necks make this acoustic great first instrument for the beginner.
